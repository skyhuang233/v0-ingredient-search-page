# User Guide

Welcome to the **Bio-Adaptive Smart Search** application! This guide will help you use the advanced features of our system to find the perfect recipes for your health goals.

## 1. Getting Started

### Prerequisites
Ensure the application is running. You should see the search interface at `http://localhost:3000`.

### Setting Your Profile
For the best experience, ensure your profile is set up. The system uses your profile to tailor recommendations (e.g., avoiding allergens, prioritizing high-protein meals for muscle gain).
*(Note: Profile management is currently handled via the settings or automatically linked to your account)*

## 2. Searching for Recipes

1.  **Enter Ingredients:**
    In the main search bar, type the ingredients you have on hand or want to eat.
    *   *Example:* "chicken, spinach, rice"

2.  **View Recommendations:**
    The system will instantly analyze thousands of recipes and present the best matches.
    *   **Top Picks:** You will see a curated list of the top 3 recipes that best match your ingredients and health goals.
    *   **Nutritionist's Note:** Look for the highlighted "Nutritionist's Note" box. This is an AI-generated explanation telling you *why* these recipes were chosen for you (e.g., "These recipes are low in sodium, matching your heart-health preference").

## 3. Understanding the Results

Each recipe card displays:
*   **Recipe Name & Image**
*   **Match Score:** How well it fits your ingredients.
*   **AI Reasoning:** A specific note on why this recipe fits your current goal (e.g., "High Fiber").

## 4. Using the AI Chat Assistant

If you have questions about a recipe or need substitution ideas:
1.  Click the **Chat** icon or open the sidebar.
2.  Ask questions like:
    *   "Can I swap spinach for kale in this recipe?"
    *   "Is this keto-friendly?"
    *   "How do I make this spicier?"
3.  The AI assistant, aware of the recipe you are viewing, will provide helpful answers.

## 5. Troubleshooting

*   **No Results?** Try entering fewer or more general ingredients.
*   **Slow Response?** The "Smart Reranking" process involves complex AI analysis, which may take a few seconds. This ensures the highest quality advice.
